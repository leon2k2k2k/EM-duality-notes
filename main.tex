\title{Topological Abelian Duality}
\author{Leon Liu}




\begin{document}
\maketitle

Abelian duality is a major theme in both mathematics and physics,
namely representation theory and quantum field theories. 
In this minicourse, we will explore a homotopic version of abelian duality through the lense of 
topological field thories. Moreover, we will see how field theories behaves like 
sheaves and generalize the abelian duality theory to more general (homotopic) situations.
